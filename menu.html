Starters

CRISPY CHILLI BABY CORN

Fried chilli baby corn is a common Indo-Chinese fusion side and snack.It features sweet and tender pieces of baby corn, battered and fried to crispy perfection.This is one more vegan and vegetarian friendly snack, with an even spicier and brighter flavor.

CHILLI CHICKEN DRY

Chilli chicken dry is a popular Indo-Chinese dish of diced crispy chicken in a spicy Chinese flavoured sauce. Succulent chicken coated in a batter and fried until crisp and then tossed in a garlicky chilli luscious sauce- one bite and you would definitely be reaching out for more.

CHILLI FISH DRY

A delicious, tangy Indo-fusion dish, chilli fish is everything an appetizer or a side dish should be. It is crunchy, tasty, filling and full of aromatic flavours to have your taste buds tantalized. It can be relished simply as an appetizer or as a side with our fried rice or noodles.

KUNG PAO SOUP

Kung Pao Soup, one of the most healthy starters, with minimum usage of oil. It is packed with fresh aromatic ingredients and classic Asian flavors giving a little extra kick to your favorite soup.

TERIYAKI CHICKEN

Deliciously authentic Teriyaki Chicken with a hint of garlic adding a twist to the traditional Japanese teriyaki chicken recipe, giving it a sweet and savory taste. Crispy skinless and boneless stir-fried chicken thighs along with offering a tangy and salty umami makes it absolutely addictive.
